FAQ

What is AI Art Masters?+
AI Art Master is a marketplace that connects you with companies that need real, original data to build and improve AI. You complete simple missions using your own device - capture something quick like a selfie or a short video, or submit photos you already have - and earn points you redeem for gift cards.
What kinds of missions are there?+
Two kinds, mostly. Quick captures - a selfie, a 15-second clip of you speaking, a photo of something around you. And gallery missions that use content you already have, like 50 photos of your cat or the clothes you wear. Surveys and annotation tasks are also available as a complementary, optional way to earn extra points between missions.
How much can I earn?+
It depends on how many missions you complete and which ones you take on. As your user score grows, you unlock advanced missions worth far more points. You'll always see the reward before you start, so there are no surprises.
How do I get paid?+
Your points collect in your AI Art Master wallet, and you can easily redeem them whenever you like for gift cards from well-known brands available in your region. Your gift card is delivered straight to your email - no payment or bank details required.
What is the user score, and why does it matter?+
Your user score reflects how well you follow mission instructions and the quality of your submissions. The closer you follow the guidance, the more likely you are to be approved and the higher your score climbs. A higher score unlocks advanced, higher-value missions, so doing great work directly leads to earning more.
Do I need any experience or special equipment?+
No. If you have a smartphone, you can start. Every mission includes clear, step-by-step guidance, so you always know exactly what to do.
Why do you ask for details like my location or languages?+
Companies request data from specific places, languages, and backgrounds. Sharing a few attributes lets us show you only the missions you're eligible for and that fit your skills and capabilities - so you never waste time on missions that aren't a fit. You stay in control of what you share.
Is my data and privacy protected?+
Yes. AI Art Master is operated by a publicly traded company, which means we follow data-protection and compliance rules closely. You choose what to submit, and your contributions are handled responsibly. Read our full privacy policy.
How long does it take to get my reward?+
We guarantee a review within 48 hours of your submission. Once a mission is approved, your points are added to your wallet and you can redeem a gift card right away - delivered straight to your email.
Where is AI Art Master available?+
AI Art Master is built for a global audience, with missions and gift-card options tailored to your country.
How do I get started?+
Sign up for free, set up your profile, and pick your first mission. You can be earning within minutes.
